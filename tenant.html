Tenants

Tech-Enabled Leasing For A Seamless Experience.

Take Your Lifestyle To The Next Level

Finding a rental property and making sure that all your necessary requirements are in accordance with the Residential Tenancy Act of 2006 and Rental Fairness Act of 2017 can be a complicated and time-consuming process. By joining Royal York Property Management, you will have access to our Leasing Department, which is dedicated to guiding you through the entire leasing process. They are available to service you 24 hours a day, 7 days a week!

We understand that your comfort, security, and peace of mind are key in being successful in renting a property. Therefore, Royal York Property Management leverages technology to make the process as simple as possible. From processing your rental payments to uploading required documents through our Online Portal, and even signing your Offer to Lease and Lease Agreement electronically, Royal York Property Management is dedicated to making the leasing process as convenient as possible. Additionally, Royal York Property Management is at the forefront of marketing in the industry, and provide virtual tours and professional photos for all of our rental properties available for lease. This gives you the ability to avoid in-person showings.

Feel free to watch our virtual tour, apply online, submit the payments required, and sign all paperwork virtually. You don't even need to pick up your keys, we will have them delivered to you!

Required Documents

Please ensure you upload clear, easy-to-read PDF files of all required documents to the Royal York Property Management document portal, within 3 days of your Offer to Lease being approved.

  • Government Issued ID
  • Social Insurance Card or 2nd Piece of Government ID
  • Credit Report with FICO Score
  • Employment Letter
  • 3 Most Recent Pay Stubs
