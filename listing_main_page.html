About 132 Clifton Pl #2

Home | Rentals | Bedford-Stuyvesant |Â 132 Clifton Pl #2 | Brooklyn | NY 11238, USA

Welcome to this extra large 2 bed 2 bath in a stunning bay-fronted limestone townhouse on the border of Clinton Hill and Bedford Stuyvesant. The kitchen features top-of-the-line stainless steel appliances, ample counter space and storage. The kitchen is south facing, filling the room with gorgeous sunlight. In the front of the unit you will find two gorgeous bedrooms. The primary offers an en-suite bathroom that includes a double vanity. There is a washer and dryer in the unit and options for private roof terrace. Located on a quiet tree-lined street, just moments away from the best that Clinton Hill and Bedford Stuyvesant have to offer.
