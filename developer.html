Full-Scale Management Solution For Your Buyers

With the majority of developments being marketed to investors, partnering with Royal York Property Management will enable your team to sell properties to investor buyers with ease. Our team will work with you and your buyers to ensure that their property is leased out quickly to a qualified tenant that is backed by our rental guarantee! Partnering with us will allow you and your team to focus on the issues that affect your core business while we take care of your buyers. 

Focus On Development

There is no reason for you and your team to get bogged down in dealing with property owners and tenants. Managing the relationship between property owners and tenants can be difficult, especially when the owner is a non-resident. Our team at Royal York Property Management is ready to assist you in offering a full management solution to your buyers. Our team will handle the leasing, rent collection, maintenance, and daily correspondence with tenants. Our team will deal with tenant matters and work with the property owners you can focus on what is important to you.

Guaranteed Rental Income

Investor buyers are often nervous about getting a bad tenant or losing rental income. With our rental guarantee, your investors can buy with confidence. If their tenant defaults or causes negligent damage, Royal York Property Management will be there to cover their losses, evict the tenant, and find them a new one at no charge. That is our guarantee!

Portal System For Owners

Transparency At Your Fingertips

Our portal system gives your clients full access to see what is going on with their investment properties. They can review tenant documents, check their portfolio balance, or review their monthly statements from any device, anywhere in the world.
